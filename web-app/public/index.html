What's the meaning of blockchain?

Blockchain is an important concept of Bitcoin. It is essentially a decentralized database. At the same time, as the underlying technology of Bitcoin, it is a string of data blocks generated by cryptography. The data block contains information on a batch of Bitcoin network transactions used to verify the validity of its information (anti-counterfeiting) and to generate the next block.

What's the meaning of machine learning?

Machine Learning (ML) is a multidisciplinary subject involving many disciplines such as probability theory, statistics, approximation theory, convex analysis, and algorithm complexity theory. Specializing in how computers simulate or implement human learning behaviors to acquire new knowledge or skills and reorganize existing knowledge structures to continuously improve their performance.

What's the meaning of insurance?

Insurance (insurance or abbreviated as insur) is intended to be a reliable and reliable guarantee; it is extended to a guarantee mechanism, a tool for planning life finance, a basic means of risk management under market economy conditions, a financial system and social security.
